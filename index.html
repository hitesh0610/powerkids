We create worlds, characters, and experiences

Powerkids is beneficially held by OL Master (Singapore Fund 1) Pte Limited and has the privilege of being supported by OCP Asia which is an established investment manager with a proven track record of providing customized financing solutions for small and medium sized enterprises across the Asia Pacific region.

Proven Financing Expertise Since 2004

It has a track record of offering credible and efficient financing structures for a wide range of purposes across the business cycle including growth, acquisition, and recapitalisation. Since 2004, the team has completed investments totalling approximatelyUS$5bn and currently manages funds more than US$2bn.

Manoj Mishra's Bio

Manoj brings incredible experience, leadership and an enviable track record of building and growing creative businesses and diverse teams in the media and animation industry all over the world. He is responsible for developing Powerkids’ overall strategy, executing upon the plan, managing the team, liaising with partners and other stakeholders and delivering attractive financial results.

Manoj has more than 25 years of expertise in kids' entertainment spanning the entire value chain including the development, commissioning, creation, acquisition and monetization of IP both via broadcast and OTT channels. In addition, he brings a wealth of domain expertise in value creation and maximisation through licensing & merchandising, rights management and building global partnerships with other content owners.

He has been instrumental in building and growing a number of successful businesses in various organizations with a long track record in leading multicultural teams both through organic growth and via mergers, acquisitions and joint ventures. He is a very hands-on leader given his deep understanding of all operational aspects of the animation industry and his deep knowledge of broadcast, digital and home-video markets across the globe.

Previously, Manoj has worked as the Chief Operating Officer of DQE group. He also has experience as the Head of IP Development/Commissioning, Co-Production and Content Acquisition at Al Jazeera children’s channel which covered 22 nations in the Middle East. His expertise in the gaming sector is via previous roles as Head of the Gaming division in Wipro Technologies and Business Manager at Pentafour Softwares.

Manoj has long-standing and deep personal relationships across the world with some of the biggest names in the media and animation space.

Manoj holds a Bachelor`s degree from Calcutta University, a Masters in Human Resource Management from IISWBM and a Post Graduate Diploma in Marketing & PR from BVB India.
